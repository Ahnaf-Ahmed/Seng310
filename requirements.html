Requirements

Students

The McPherson Library is often the main place of study/ meet up for uvic students therefore they are the most frequent users of the study rooms. Throughout the semester the library faces periods of high traffic which can conflict with student needs. Most students will find booking through this study room site to be more convenient and reduces unnecessary stress incurred from the current UVic site. Theyâ€™d find the notification, share, and mesh features to be especially helpful.

Tutors

The library is frequently used by tutors helping out other students. The study rooms give a more secluded space to allow for open discussion and whiteboards to show ideas. Tutors are generally older students helping younger students who might be newer to UVic and unfamiliar with its systems. Tutors need to be able to help these newer students find the study room as well so a visual display showing rooms as well as being able to send room info would be immensely helpful.

Admins

Administrators cover various types of people affiliated with the UVic library. A major part of this group are workshop coordinators for the digital scholarship commons or any other UVic affiliated group. For them it would be important if they need more secluded spaces for parts of their workshop giving students a more 1 on 1 experience. Another member of this group are librarians who maintain the study rooms wanting to make sure there is a proper level of fairness involved as well as making it open to new students to use.
