User Profiles

Stan

Stan is a 3rd year psychopathology student. Stan is extremely adept at utilizing tools he is already been familiarized with, such as his everyday data modelling and visualization software, but is often lost when first exposed to desktop and web applications. Combining his learning comprehension and patience, Stan's recipe for success is unfaltering perseverance and his love for documentation. Though beyond getting applications to suit his needs, he doesn't try to understand how they work. Stan is often found studying in the same spot of the first floor of the library, but wishes there was reliable seating.

Raphael

Raphael is a workshop coordinator that books rooms in the library if they're needed for his client's workshops. Raphael is familiar with UVic technology as he's used it for a number of years. However that is generally the only kind he expects to use as a coordinator and has certain expectations for how a program should work. As such if a program deviates from what he expects he can get easily lost and may downright refuse to use it as the "older way is easier and faster to use." As he works with UVic he is given a level of priority over usage of certain rooms as well as book for longer periods of time.

Daniel

Daniel is a math and physics tutor who mostly works with university students. He has used computers but only when needed which isn’t often. He is familiar with the common tasks of checking email and the weather but hasn’t spent ample time browsing the internet or navigating UVic’s labyrinth of a website. He prefers to do research the old school way of going to the library and taking a book out.
