Integration with UVic Website

Requirement #6

Description: The study room web app should be easily accessed from UVics' online tools dropdown menu and should notify students with a link if they have upcoming due dates or tests.

Scenario

Stan is looking at coursespaces and sees that she has a group project deliverable due on Monday morning. Wanting to make sure that his group has time to meet up he clicks a link on the side and it sends him straight to the study room booking page.
