Stakeholder Goals

Students

Students will be able to book study rooms for themselves, or on behalf of their group. Students will have access to additional functionalities as well, including deleting or moving their room appointments (provided the room is available and can fit the group) and determining room capacity. Students can only reserve one room at any given time, but the student or group may reserve the room for as long as they like, allowing people like Stan to secure their favourite study location in the library.

Tutors

Extending their capabilities from the Students' privileges, a tutor will be able to reserve multiple rooms at any given time in order to schedule their upcoming tutoring sessions. Tutors may also have access to larger rooms, such as the MATH & STAT centre on the first floor of the library, and can bypass the Minimum Group of Three requirement.

Admins

Administrators will be able to effectively manage the Study Room software and help support the userbase. Administrators will be able to remove a group from a room in the event of misuse, and, if necessary, block select users from reserving rooms. In addition, admins can set/edit room size limits, move groups around, enable/disable rooms, and edit a user's role (Student, Tutor, etc.)
